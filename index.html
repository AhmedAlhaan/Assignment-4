Recipe of the Day: Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Itâ€™s simple, delicious, and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • 50g Parmesan
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps to Prepare

  • Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  • Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  • Beat the eggs and mix with grated cheeses.
  • Drain the spaghetti and mix with pancetta.
  • Remove from heat and stir in the egg and cheese mixture.
  • Season with black pepper and serve immediately.
